edant.io

Edant is an organization created for the love of software. We celebrate automation and revel in the art of computer programming. We write code for clouds and wire transistors all the same. We deliver beautiful software.

Edant is the best part of Ubuntu and Arch Linux: friendly and focused. We KISS as a matter of course. Edant is Bjarne Stroustrup walking into a bar with Linus Torvalds. We contribute to the ISO C++ Study Groups SG15 and SG20. We enjoy reading specifications and creating distributed networks. We create servers from scratch in C++ and render webpages with webassembly. We are fluent and efficient.

Our code is an arrangement of best practices and core guidelines. Our binaries are analyzed, sanitized and profiled. Our software is stored in a private package manager. We value our work.

Edant is the harmony of good software and good people. Get in touch.
